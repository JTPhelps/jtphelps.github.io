About us

We are the Atkins High School varsity baseball team. Since 2022, our team has played in the Mid-State 3A conference and has regularly
in the hunt for a conference title. Last year our team made the NCHSAA 3A state playoffs for the first time in school history.
Led by all-conference players Craig Belden and Bryce Mabe the 2024 squad hopes to win the Mid-State 3A and make a return to the NCHSAA 3A playoffs.

Fundraiser

The Atkins High School baseball team our annual 2023 winter BBQ funraiser. Camels sold barbecue by the pound to raise money
for the team's spring break tournament in eastern North Carolina. Last year's team sold over $2000 of barbecue and was able to preform much
needed field repairs. Even though our 2023 fundraiser is over, the team is still accepting donations here
