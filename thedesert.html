Our home field advantage: The Desert

Located at 3605 Old Greensboro Road , Joe Childress Memorial Baseball Field, nicknamed "The Desert" by the camel faithful, has been the home of the Atkins Fighting Camels since the 2006 season!.
In its storied 18 seasons The Desert has seen over 160 varsity baseball games! It's nickname was coined by the class of 2023 seniors Angelo, Marcello, and Andre.
The class of 2023 felt the nickname fit because "In The Desert we play hard, we play fair, and we make every pitch count. It is a tough place to be the visiting team."
The Camels will open their 2024 campaign @ The Desert on 2/26 against Mount Airy HS @7PM
